WELCOME

Welcome to my webpage. I am a Faculty member at the National Institute for Nigerian Languages, Aba, Nigeria where I teach courses in Linguistics, English and Urhobo. My current research interests include: Phonology, Sociolinguistics, Ethnography and Language documentation.

I hold BA and MA degrees in Linguistics from Delta State University, Abraka, and the University of Benin respectively. I am currently a PhD (Linguistics) candidate at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka.



Projects

  • Urhobo Bibliography

    A Collection of Research Works on Different Aspects of the Urhobo Language and People.

  • Erohwa Wordlists

    A wordlist of Erohwa, an endangered langguage spoken by the Erohwa people of Isoko South in Delta State and Sagbama in Bayelsa State.

  • Thomas' Collections

    A Project on Northcote Whitridge Thomas' (1868-1936) Anthropological Collections on the Urhobo People.
