ABOUT


​Faculty member, Department of Linguistics, National Institute for Nigerian Languages, Aba, Nigeria.

BA (Linguistics), Delta State University, Abraka;
MA (Linguistics), University of Benin, Nigeria;
PhD (Linguistics), Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka.
​
Research Interests: Phonetics/Phonology, Sociolinguistics, Ehnography, Language documentation
